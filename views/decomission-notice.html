The Origami Registry has been decomissioned

The Origami Registry is being replaced by Storybook and is no longer available. Please refer to Storybook for Origami component demos. You can read more about the decomission on our blog.

What do I do next?

  • You may have found this page through a link in documentation. Please ensure your documentation is updated to point to the equivalent demo in Storybook.
  • Visit our Storybook website for all Origami demos.
  • Is there something missing? Please let us know in #origami-support if there is something missing that was previously available in the registry.
